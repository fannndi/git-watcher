# Privacy Policy for TI24A4 App 32 (Git Watcher)

**Last Updated: May 16, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how TI24A4 App 32 ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Local Data Storage
The App is designed to respect your privacy. All information you provide, including:
- **GitHub Usernames**
- **Repository Lists**
- **GitHub Personal Access Tokens (if provided)**

is stored **locally on your device** using secure local storage. We do not host any servers and do not collect this data on our own infrastructure.

### GitHub API Integration
To provide its core functionality (watching repository commits), the App communicates directly with the **GitHub API**. 
- Your GitHub username and/or Personal Access Token are used solely to authenticate requests to GitHub to fetch commit data.
- This data is transmitted securely via HTTPS to GitHub's servers.

## 2. Permissions
The App requires the following permissions to function:
- **Internet Access:** To fetch commit data from GitHub.
- **Notifications:** To alert you when new commits are detected in your watched repositories.
- **Background Execution:** To check for updates even when the app is not actively in use.
- **Battery Optimization Exemption:** To ensure reliable background synchronization.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:
- **Google Play Services** (for App distribution and updates)
- **GitHub API** (for fetching repository data)

Please refer to the privacy policies of these third-party providers for more information.

## 4. Children's Privacy
These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age.

## 5. Security
We value your trust in providing us your information. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security. Since your data is stored locally, the security of your data depends on the security of your device.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
